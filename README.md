### Task 5 — Cybercrime Awareness Case Study & Prevention Guide

**Part A – Case Study: UPI/Online Payment Fraud**  
A detailed case study of the "Collect Request" scam targeting college students on OLX/Quikr — how a student lost ₹18,000 by entering their UPI PIN after being tricked into thinking they were "receiving" a payment. Includes a 7-step breakdown of the fraud method.

> Key insight: **You never need to enter your UPI PIN to receive money. A PIN is only required to send or authorise a debit.**

**Part B – Prevention Checklist: Stay Safe Online**  
15 actionable tips for college students covering general safety, UPI-specific fraud prevention, social media safety, and what to do if victimised.

🆘 Report cybercrime: **[cybercrime.gov.in] (https://cybercrime.gov.in)** | Helpline: **1930** (24x7)
